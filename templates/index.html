HOW IT WORKS

Seven signals. Seven anchors.

Every signal cites a public standard. Composite verdict by transparent rule.

01 · TERRITORY
Indigenous-land overlap
Native Land Digital + bundled overlay fallback. Returns the polygons the project sits on.
UNDRIP · ILO 169
02 · MEDIA
Adverse news (topic + claim)
GDELT 2.0 + Google News with cascading query variants. Article scores adverse only when a safeguards topic and an adverse-claim term co-occur.
ICVCM CCP #5
03 · LITIGATION
Active climate cases
Sabin Center Climate Case Chart + inferred from news.
Sabin Center · IPCC AR6 WG3 Ch.15
04 · NGO
Investigative complaints
Curated ledger (CMW, Survival, Forest Peoples) plus auto-promoted news from those outlets.
ICVCM CCP #5
05 · ENVIRONMENTAL
Forest-cover trend
World Bank AG.LND.FRST.ZS, 5-yr window. RED ≤ −0.3 pp/yr per FAO threshold.
World Bank · FAO FRA 2020
06 · GOVERNANCE
TI CPI bands
Bands anchored to TI 2024 global avg of 43. <30 = TI's "serious corruption." 30–42 = below global avg. ≥43 = at/above.
TI CPI 2024
07 · FPIC CHECKLIST
Procedural 4-check
Consultation · Consent · Grievance mechanism · Withdrawal protections. Each returns pass / fail / insufficient / not applicable, anchored to the UN-REDD operational FPIC checklist.
UNDRIP · ILO 169 · Cancun Safeguards

THE ENGINE

Not a fixture. An engine.

Multi-tier resolver — every external call has a graceful fallback, every result is shape-stable, every input is validated. Exercised across 94 unit + integration tests plus 15 live blind screens on previously unseen projects: zero crashes.

Northern Rangelands Trust carbon program (Kenya) — not in the curated ledger, never seen by the engine before — returned RED 11 with three adverse articles surfaced from cold news inference: Mongabay ("Kenyan soil carbon project suspended for a second time"), Survival International ("Controversial carbon credits scheme used by Netflix and Meta suspended AGAIN"), and FIDH ("human rights abuses against Indigenous"). That's the proof: this works for projects no one told it about.
